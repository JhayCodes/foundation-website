School Supplies Outreach

Providing essential learning materials to children in under-resourced primary schools.

The Problem

Many children attend school without basic learning materials, which negatively affects their learning experience and motivation.

Our Solution

We distributed school bags, books, pens, and other essentials to students in selected primary schools, ensuring they have the tools they need to learn effectively.

Impact So Far

  • 120 students supported
  • 3 primary schools reached
  • 15 volunteers involved

What’s Next

We aim to expand this initiative to more schools and include mentorship sessions alongside material support.

Project Details

Location: Accra, Ghana

Category: Education

Status: Ongoing

Start Date: March 2025
